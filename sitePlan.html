Site Plan - Desert Explorers Hub

Site Name

Desert Explorers Hub
This name reflects a community-driven platform for outdoor adventurers and hikers interested in desert landscapes and trails. It evokes a sense of exploration, resilience, and connection to nature.

Optional Domain: desertexplorershub.org

Site Purpose

The site will provide curated desert trail guides, survival tips, equipment recommendations, and allow visitors to join the community through membership. The site will also feature weather info and maps for desert hiking and camping.

Scenarios

  • What gear should I bring for a 3-day hike in the Mojave Desert?
  • Are there any beginner-friendly desert trails near Phoenix, AZ?

Color Schema

Primary Color: Desert Orange
Used for headings, highlights, and accent borders.

Secondary Color: Sand White
Used as background color and for body text areas for a light, airy feel.

Typography

  • Bebas Neue - Used for headings and titles to provide a strong, clean desert look.
  • Roboto - Used for paragraph and body text for readability and clean presentation.
