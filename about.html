Who We Are

Founded in the heart of the desert, São Gabriel Butcher Shop has proudly served the community with high-quality, fresh meat since 1998. We believe in tradition, quality, and treating every customer like family.

Our Story

What started as a small family butcher shop has grown into a trusted name for premium meats in the region. We work with local farms to ensure our products are always fresh, sustainable, and responsibly sourced.

Our Values

  • 🌱 Local & sustainable sourcing
  • 🤝 Honest service and fair pricing
  • 🔪 Butchery as a craft
