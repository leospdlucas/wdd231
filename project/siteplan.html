Saint Gabriel Butcher Shop — Website Planning Document

E‑commerce plan

Site Name

Proposed Name: Saint Gabriel Butcher Shop

  • Connected to the local brand.
  • This is a local, traditional, family-run butcher shop that has changed ownership three times in 40 years and has never changed its trade name.

Domain ideas (availability not checked): Saintgabrielbutcher.com.br, Saintgabrielmeats.com.br

Site Purpose

This site will be the storefront and sales channel for Saint Gabriel Butcher Shop. It includes a product catalog, barbecue bundles, weekly deals, and simple ordering for pickup or delivery (WhatsApp and delivery drivers).

  • Catalog with price per kg, cut options (thin/medium/thick steak), and daily availability.
  • Store page with address, map, hours (including holidays), and contact channels.
  • Orders: simple cart + quick WhatsApp link with an order summary.

Scenarios

  • "What is today's price per kg for picanha? Do you have barbecue bundles?"
  • "Do you deliver outside the city? What is the fee and minimum order?"
  • "Can I choose my steak thickness (thin/medium/thick)?"

Wireframe — Home Layout

Simple boxes show layout only. Two breakpoints: mobile (single column) and desktop (splitting layout). Based on our saved pattern (Header • Hero • Data Panel • Side Panel • Footer), adapted for e‑commerce.

Mobile (≤ 480–640px)
Header — Brand • Cart • WhatsApp
Hero — Deal Banner
Catalog — Products / Filters / Search
Side Panel — Bundles, Delivery, Loyalty
Footer — Address, Hours, Last Updated
Desktop (≥ 1000px)
Header — Brand • Nav • Cart
Hero — Promotional Banner (full‑width)
Left — Product Grid / Filters
Right — Bundles, Delivery and WhatsApp CTA
Footer — Address, Hours, Social, Last Updated

CSS Notes

  • CSS variables define the color scheme and can switch for campaigns (e.g., barbecue season).
  • Mobile‑first structure in styles/site.css, large‑screen upgrades in styles/site-large.css.
  • Planned components: product cards, responsive grid, sale badges, add‑to‑cart buttons, and a quick WhatsApp link.
