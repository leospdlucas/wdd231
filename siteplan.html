New Timbuktu Chamber of Commerce

Individual Project Site Plan

Site Name

Proposed Site Name: New Timbuktu Chamber of Commerce

Rationale: This name clearly communicates that the website represents the official chamber of commerce for the fictional city of New Timbuktu. It emphasizes the geographic identity (“New Timbuktu”) and the purpose of the organization (“Chamber of Commerce”), supporting professional branding, credibility, and easy recognition by local businesses, investors, and community members.

Optional domain idea: newtimbuktuchamber.org

Site Purpose

The New Timbuktu Chamber of Commerce website serves as a central hub for businesses, entrepreneurs, residents, and visitors in New Timbuktu. The site will:

  • Provide a searchable member directory highlighting local businesses and services.
  • Promote upcoming networking events, workshops, and community activities.
  • Offer information on membership levels, benefits, and how to join the chamber.
  • Share economic highlights, spotlights on featured businesses, and local news.
  • Support potential investors and new residents with basic relocation and contact information.

Overall, the site aims to strengthen the local business ecosystem, facilitate connections, and communicate the value of chamber membership.

Scenarios

The following scenarios represent common questions that a target visitor might have when they access the site:

  • Scenario 1: “I own a small business in New Timbuktu. How can I join the chamber, and what are the differences between the membership levels?”
  • Scenario 2: “I am visiting New Timbuktu and want to shop locally. Where can I find a list of recommended member businesses and their contact information?”
  • Scenario 3: “As an entrepreneur, I want to know what networking events, workshops, or expos the chamber is hosting in the upcoming months.”
  • Scenario 4: “I am considering relocating my company to New Timbuktu. How can I contact the chamber and learn more about the local business environment?”

Color Scheme

The color palette is inspired by warm, golden tones that suggest opportunity, prosperity, and a welcoming business environment. These colors are already used in the current project and will remain consistent across the chamber site.

Primary
Hex: #e3b23c
Usage: Header background, primary buttons, highlights, and key UI elements.
Accent
Hex: #f3a712
Usage: Call-to-action buttons (e.g., “Join the Chamber”), hover states, and emphasis borders.
Background
Hex: #fff3cd
Usage: Overall page background and subtle card backgrounds for a warm, light feel.
Body Text
Hex: #333333
Usage: Main body text and most headings to ensure readability and contrast.
Dark Mode Background
Hex: #1a1a1a
Usage: Dark mode header and page background when the theme toggle is active.

These colors will be consistently applied to the homepage, directory, join, and discover pages to reinforce the chamber’s visual identity and improve brand recognition.

Typography

The site will use a clean, system-friendly sans-serif font stack to ensure readability and fast loading across devices. The current project uses Arial, sans-serif and this will remain the primary typeface.

Primary Heading Font
Arial, sans-serif – Headings (h1–h3)

Used for the main site title, section headings, and card titles to provide a clear and professional look.

Body Text Font

Arial, sans-serif – body copy, navigation links, form labels, event descriptions, and directory details for consistent, easy-to-read text.

Additional emphasis (such as bold text or slightly increased font size) will be used instead of extra decorative fonts to keep the interface clean and performance-friendly.
